Frequently Asked Questions

What is CAT Analytics Studio, and how does it help my preparation?

CAT Analytics Studio is a data-driven performance tracking platform. It goes beyond basic scores to analyze your mock test results, identify hidden conceptual gaps, track time management, and provide personalized, actionable insights to help you maximize your percentile.

How do I sync or import my mock test scores into the dashboard?
You can log your data in three ways:
  • Auto-Sync: Use our secure browser extension to pull results directly from major test series portals.
  • PDF Upload: Drag and drop your official mock scorecard PDF.
  • Manual Entry: Manually input your raw sectional scores, attempts, and accuracy.
How is the "Predicted Percentile" calculated?

Our analytics engine normalizes your raw scores by comparing them against historical CAT data, slot difficulty levels, and the performance patterns of thousands of other aspirants on the platform to give you a highly realistic percentile estimate.

How can I benchmark my performance against mock toppers?

Every mock analysis page includes a Topper Overlay Graph. This allows you to visually compare your time allocation, question-skipping strategy, and accuracy tier against the top 1% and 99th percentile scorers.

What is the AI-Powered Error Tracker?

It’s a smart log that categorizes your incorrect answers into three buckets: Silly Mistakes (calculation/reading errors), Conceptual Gaps (lack of core knowledge), or Time-Pressure Panics. This helps you avoid repeating the same mistakes in your next mock.

Is my mock data and test history secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption to safeguard your performance metrics and profile information. Your personal dashboard data is entirely private and is never shared with third parties or other coaching institutes.

My mock metrics aren't updating or look incomplete. What should I do?

Try clearing your browser cache and refreshing the page. If a recent mock import didn't process fully, go to your dashboard, click "Re-sync Mock Data," and wait a few moments. For persistent issues, drop us a line at iimabc2003@gmail.com
